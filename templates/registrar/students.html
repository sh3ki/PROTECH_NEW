School Biometric Data Policy

Introduction

This policy outlines how our school collects, uses, stores, and protects biometric data (facial recognition) for student attendance tracking and campus security purposes.

1. Data Collection

We collect facial biometric data through:

  • Three photographs (front, left profile, right profile) during enrollment
  • Mathematical templates derived from facial features (not the actual photos)
  • Original photos are not stored after template generation

2. Purpose and Use

Your biometric data will be used exclusively for:

  • Automated attendance tracking (time-in/time-out)
  • Campus security and access control
  • Generating attendance reports for parents and administrators

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All biometric templates are encrypted and stored on secure servers
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and backups are performed
  • Data is not shared with third parties

4. Data Retention

Biometric data will be retained for the duration of student enrollment and will be permanently deleted within 30 days after graduation or withdrawal from the school.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You may request access to view your biometric data
  • You may request correction of inaccurate data
  • You may request deletion of your data (this will affect attendance tracking)
  • You may withdraw consent at any time

6. Legal Compliance

This policy complies with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and other applicable Philippine laws regarding the collection and processing of biometric information.

7. Contact Information

For questions or concerns about this policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at the school administration office.

Pro Tips:

  • Leave ID blank for new students (ID is auto-generated)
  • LRN must be exactly 12 digits and unique across all students
  • Grade and Section must exist in the system and match each other
  • Status must be either ACTIVE or INACTIVE (case-sensitive)
  • Guardian fields are optional, but if provided, first name, last name, phone, and relationship are required
  • Middle Name and Alignment are optional for both students and guardians

Important Notes

  • Existing Records: If the ID column contains an existing student ID, that row will be automatically skipped to prevent duplicates.
  • LRN Validation: LRN must be exactly 12 digits and unique across all students.
  • Grade-Section Matching: The section must belong to the specified grade. Mismatched combinations will be rejected.
  • Guardian Data: If any guardian field is provided, first name, last name, phone, and relationship become required.
  • Validation: All data is validated before import. If errors are found, the entire import will be cancelled and you'll see detailed error messages.
